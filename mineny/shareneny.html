We All Have Something To Share

Share is according to the dictionary: A part or portion of a larger amount that is divided among a number of people, or contributed to by a number of people. In Spanish it is a little different, give part of what you have so that someone else can enjoy it with you. What is sharing in psychology? Sharing is a prosocial behavior and is the joint use of a resource or space. The definition in Spanish is: To give a part or portion of what we have, to enjoy it together with whom we share. Sharing is giving someone a part of what we have, and enjoy it together.
