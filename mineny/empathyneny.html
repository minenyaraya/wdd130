Empathy

We Can Support Others

Empathy is according to the dictionary: the ability to understand and share the feelings of another. Feeling empathy is recognizing and feeling what another person is going through, because we have had the same experiences, or because we have the natural gift of observing and understanding other people. This is your place, where sharing is fun, and people can interact to understand each other.
