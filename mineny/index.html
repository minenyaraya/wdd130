The Treasure of Learning

This is a website for co-workers, families, or individuals. Being unique makes us different. Being different can make us go out of tune. We want to emphasize good communication and find harmony in it. To learn is one of the human abilities is learning, from babbling to speaking, from crawling to walking. Because they are natural facts, we forget to see that everything we learn is an achievement, a miracle, a consequence of having perseverance, it is an act of faith, it is having the courage to do it, having the will, it is the right thing to do. A good use of time is to make the most of life's opportunities. Now, what is learning? Let's see what the dictionary says: To learn is to gain or acquire knowledge or skill in (something) through study, experience, or teaching. The word learn in spanish "Aprender"comes from the Latin • Ap- which means close to and –prehendere, to capture. What it means is that we are close to capturing something, the list of what we can capture is long.
